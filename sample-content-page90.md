## BONE DISEASES


### Osteoporosis Is A Significant Health Care Burden

Osteoporosis is a chronic condition that results in low bone mass and strength,
microarchitectural deterioration, and an increase in fractures. All bones become more
fragile and susceptible to fracture as the condition progresses, with the vertebrae, wrist,
hip, pelvis, humerus, clavicle, femur, and tibia/fibula accounting for the vast majority of
all fractures.
